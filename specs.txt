# Build a Family Oral History App called 'Remember When'

Create a simple web app for recording my father's life story through an AI-guided interview.

## Goal

The app should preserve a lifetime of memories by asking thoughtful follow-up questions based on previous answers. The experience should feel like an intelligent interviewer, not a static questionnaire.

## Core User Flow

1. Present the user with the first five foundational questions.
2. The user records an audio answer (up to 5 minutes) for each question.
3. The recording is saved as an MP3.
4. Upload the MP3 to Google Cloud Storage.
5. Transcribe the audio.
6. Send the transcript, conversation history, and biographical context to an LLM.
7. The LLM generates the single best follow-up question.
8. Repeat indefinitely, allowing the interview to go as deep as desired into any topic.

## Storage

For each response, store:

* Question
* Transcript
* MP3 URL (Google Cloud Storage)
* Timestamp
* Parent question ID
* Conversation/thread ID
* AI-generated metadata (optional)

The conversation should form a tree rather than a flat list so each answer can branch into deeper memories.

## Recording

* Maximum recording length: 5 minutes
* One-click Record / Stop
* Playback before saving
* Re-record if desired
* Upload automatically after confirmation

## AI Interviewing

The AI should behave like an experienced oral historian.

It should:

* Ask one question at a time.
* Prefer open-ended questions.
* Follow emotional or historically significant threads.
* Avoid repeating previous questions.
* Dig deeper whenever an interesting memory appears.
* Prioritize stories over facts.
* Occasionally return to unanswered areas of life.

## Initial Questions

Begin with these five questions:

1. When you look back over your life, what are the moments that made you who you are?
2. What are your earliest memories of growing up in North Dakota and your family?
3. What do you remember most about your parents, and what did they teach you?
4. How did your years at Kodak shape your life, both professionally and personally?
5. If your children, grandchildren, and great-grandchild could only know a handful of lessons from your life, what would you want them to remember?

## Tech Stack

* React + TypeScript
* Tailwind CSS
* Google Cloud Storage for audio
* Speech-to-text transcription
* OpenAI Responses API (or equivalent LLM API) for interview generation
* Simple database (SQLite, Supabase, or Firestore) to store the conversation tree

The UI should be intentionally simple and optimized for an older adult: large text, large buttons, minimal distractions, and a clear indication of progress through the interview.
